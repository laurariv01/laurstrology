About Compatability

Just to be perfectly clear, any relationship can work. All relationships will require effort. Compatability doesn't say you can't be with someone just because of their sign. Love whoever you want! Compatability readings show us that some relationships will require more effort than others. That's all. It will show you what you can work on, and how you may communicate better with your love. Compatability reading is a tool for navigating your relationship. Enjoy!
